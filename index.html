FEATURES

YouTube to MP3.
In your DAW.

CrateDigger is the only VST plugin that lets you download audio from YouTube for free. Drag your samples right into your DAW to flip, chop, and warp as you please. We're working hard on new features for version 2. If you have feedback, reach out to us here.

More than
meets the eye.

With CrateDigger, YouTube becomes an unlimited sampling resource. Sample breakbeats, 70s soul records, video games, soundbites, field recordings, birds chirping, acapellas... is it hitting you yet? With CrateDigger, there are no limits to the samples you can use in your music.
