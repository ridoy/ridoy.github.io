Take a look at some of my projects:

Cut Up (version 0.2) [2022]

A text randomizer designed to combat writer's block, inspired by David Bowie's famous "cut-up" method for writing lyrics.

Worldwide Book Society [2022]

An experimental online book club. On WBS, you can annotate and read a wide variety of short stories. After each reading period our little clique gets together to discuss within our Discord community.

Pokemon Showdown God Mode [2022]

An inline damage calculator for Pokemon Showdown (a popular online battle simulator). Displays the potential damage your moves and your opponent's moves can do, thus eliminating a lot of the guesswork involved in competitive gameplay.

Urbit Alpha [2022]

An Urbit chatbot for on-demand financial data, like screenshots of the BTC/USD chart. Built this with my pal Tomas.

Discord React Spammer [2020]

A terrific tool if being irritating is one of your goals.

Crate (Discontinued) [2021]

A music software plugin for ripping audio straight from YouTube. Designed for music producers who make frequent use of samples in their work.
