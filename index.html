FEATURES

YouTube to MP3.
In your DAW.

Crate is the only VST plugin that lets you download audio from YouTube. Drag samples right into your DAW to flip, chop, and warp as you please. Compatible with FL Studio, Ableton, Reaper, and Logic.

More than
meets the eye.

With Crate, YouTube becomes a powerful sample library. Sample anything from breakbeats, 70s soul records, video games, soundbites, field recordings, birds chirping, acapellas and more!
